Combat

Combat in earthbound may seem simple at first, but it includes a rolling HP system where HP has to gradually scroll down to 0 instead of losing all of your health right away. This allows you to heal yourself quickly to avoid falling in battle. The game also includes fun spells and being able to one shot enemies by attacking them from the back. Grinding has never been easier!

Click here to see an example of the battles in Earthbound! »

Setting

Earthbound is one of the few RPGS to take place in modern times! Instead of fighting wizards and dragons you fight enemies like crows and insane cultists. It's another part of what makes this game so unique compared to other games.

Earthbound's fanbase is incredibly passionate.

Despite only one of the games seeing a release in North America until 2015, the fanbase for this title was passionate and fans still support it to this day. One fan in partiuclar has created several fan animations based on different scenes from each of the three games.

The music is incredible and takes inspiration from popular music artists.

Earthbound is home to some of the greatest video game music tracks of all time. Some songs even take inspiration from songs such as Johhny be good. Heck, the name of the franchise was even named Mother because of the John lennon song of the same name!
